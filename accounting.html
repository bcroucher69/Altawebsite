Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about restaurant back-office automation.

How does the POS integration work for accounting?

Altametrics pulls sales and labor data directly from your POS every night. It automatically generates journal entries, reconciles cash deposits, and updates your P&L so you have a true "Daily P&L" without manual work.

Do I need to change my current bank to use the automation?

No. We integrate with over 15,000 financial institutions globally. You simply connect your existing accounts via our secure banking portal to enable automated transaction matching and reconciliation.

Is this suitable for a single location, or is it only for chains?

While our system is powerful enough for global enterprise chains with thousands of locations, its modular nature makes it extremely efficient for single-unit operators looking to eliminate back-office busywork and focus on hospitality.

Which accounting software does Altametrics connect to?

Altametrics has certified integrations with QuickBooks, Xero, Oracle NetSuite, Sage Intacct, Microsoft Dynamics, and more. Our two-way sync pushes journal entries, vendor bills, and bank reconciliation data directly into your GL, eliminating all manual data transfer.

How does the accounts payable approval workflow function?

Once an invoice is captured and coded by our AI, it is automatically routed to the designated approver based on your custom rules (e.g., invoices over $500 require a Director's approval). Approvers receive a mobile notification, review the attached invoice image, and approve or reject with a single tap — all with a full audit trail.
