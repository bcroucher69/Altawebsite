Inventory FAQ

How does the offline inventory counting work?
Our mobile app caches your entire order guide and count sheets locally. You can count in walk-in freezers or basements without Wi-Fi, and the system will automatically sync your data the moment you reconnect.
Can we integrate our existing vendors?
Yes, Altametrics integrates with 500+ major food and beverage vendors. We support EDI, email, and portal-based ordering to ensure your supply chain is fully automated.
How accurate is the AI demand forecasting?
Our AI engine analyzes historical sales, local events, weather patterns, and holidays to predict inventory needs with up to 98% accuracy, ensuring you never over-order or run out of product.
