Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Altametrics platform and our enterprise restaurant management software.

How does Altametrics AI optimize restaurant labor costs?

Our AI continuously analyzes historical sales, foot traffic, local events, and weather to predict exactly how many staff members you need for every shift, ensuring compliance and preventing costly overstaffing while maintaining customer service standards.

Does Altametrics integrate with my existing POS and major suppliers?

Yes, Altametrics has deep, out-of-the-box API integrations with the industry's leading POS systems (including NCR Aloha, Oracle Micros, Toast, and Par) and major food suppliers (Sysco, US Foods) for seamless AP automation and real-time operational data synchronization.

Is the software built for single locations or multi-unit enterprise chains?

Altametrics is purposely built to scale for multi-unit enterprise restaurants and large franchise groups. The system handles thousands of individual stores, offering consolidated global reporting, robust hierarchy management, and enterprise-wide recipe and menu publishing.

How does the predictive inventory system reduce food waste?

By calculating theoretical versus actual usage down to the individual ingredient level in real-time. The platform generates automated Purchase Orders based on predictive sales depletion, preventing both critical stockouts and excessive spoilage of perishable goods.
