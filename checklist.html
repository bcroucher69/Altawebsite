Foundation of Food Safety

The 7 Principles of HACCP

ZipHACCP automates the internationally recognized Hazardous Analysis Critical Control Point standards for your restaurant.

Principle 1 Hazard Analysis
Principle 1

Hazard Analysis

Identify biological, chemical, or physical hazards that could contaminate food.

Principle 2 Critical Control Points
Principle 2

Critical Control Points

Determine the points where hazards can be prevented, eliminated, or reduced to safe levels.

Principle 3 Critical Limits
Principle 3

Critical Limits

Establish minimum and maximum values (like temperature) for each CCP to ensure safety.

Principle 4 Monitoring Procedures
Principle 4

Monitoring Procedures

Automated and manual tracking to ensure all critical limits are consistently met.

Principle 5 Corrective Actions
Principle 5

Corrective Actions

Define immediate steps to be taken when a critical limit is not reached.

Principle 6 Verification
Principle 6

Verification

Regular review of the HACCP system to ensure it is operating effectively.

Principle 7 Record Keeping
Principle 7

Record Keeping

Digital logs of all safety data for audit-ready compliance reporting.

Compliance FAQs

Common questions about digital food safety and ZipHACCP integration.

What are the 7 principles of HACCP?

HACCP is a management system in which food safety is addressed through the analysis and control of biological, chemical, and physical hazards from raw material production, procurement and handling, to manufacturing, distribution and consumption of the finished product. The 7 principles include: Conduct a Hazard Analysis, Determine CCPs, Establish Critical Limits, Establish Monitoring Procedures, Establish Corrective Actions, Establish Verification Procedures, and Establish Record-keeping.

Does the Bluetooth probe work with any smartphone?

Yes, the Zip HACCP Bluetooth Probe is compatible with most iOS and Android devices. It connects via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and syncs instantly with the ZipHACCP mobile application without the need for manual pairing on each use.

Is the system audit-ready for the health department?

Absolutely. ZipHACCP is built to exceed FDA and local health department standards. During an audit, you can instantly export digital logs, corrective action histories, and maintenance records, eliminating the need for bulky paper binders.
